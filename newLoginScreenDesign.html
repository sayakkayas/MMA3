About Muscle Memory Authentication

Our project aims to utilize the Myo Gesture Armband to create an authentication device that is premised on muscle memory. Our research indicates that muscle memory is unique to each individual and through the utilization of muscles as an authenticator, it can improve both security measures as well as be more convenient for the user. For more information, visit our website at http://sarmishthapv.wixsite.com/mmauthenticator
